Our Goal

Is to interview candidates for contractor employment - special people for hire that love to be recorded and look great on video. These normal everyday people are unique in the fact that they can be engaging, fun, and uniquely friendly.

These candidates are fine dressers, clear spoken, of any race or color, can speak fluently at least 1 or 2 languages, enjoy making money on their own time and energy, realize some jobs are meant to be simple, and they like the idea of steady income.

Imagine

You’re on your way out for the evening and you look like a million dollars. Suddenly, you get a message from GSMFreelancer.com - asking you to record 1 or more questions. You look at the questions in your message and put your finger on the list and copy them to your clipboard.

Then you simply open a telepromptor app, go to the script area and paste in the questions. Then using the telepromptor and reading the questions as if you’re asking the questions in a real interview with your best friend, or someone of interest - you save each recorded question, one at a time in your photo library.

How You’ll Do It Every Day - In Your Schedule NOT Ours

Using your digital video recording devices (cell phones, iPads, computers, or digital cameras) - take time out of your day to record yourself (looking directly into your device's camera) asking "requested" questions received from GSMFreelancer. Then log in to your GSMFreelancer.com account (if hired) and upload each individual "question" recording from your device's photo library. A simple and rewarding way to make a little money.
