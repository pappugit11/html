Our Story

Always Wild. Sustainable. Delivered.

The Inspiration

From the beginning

From the beginning Wild Fishermen Direct Seafood centers around the founder Baxter Cox's family history, upbringing, and his strong desire to rediscover the value of genuine food. Due to the worldwide industrial food system, the crucial bond between food and community has been severed. In order to restore sustainable meal options and enhance the food system, Fishermen Direct Seafood was created.

Fishermen Direct Seafood functions as a community-based seafood company that links ethical consumers with sustainable fishermen. We provide fresh, wild-caught seafood straight from the source, delivered straight to your doorstep. Our primary objective is to reconnect people with real food and with one another, as well as to enhance the global food system.

OUR COMPANY

A tradition within a family

Growing up on a commercial fishing boat in Alaska, I was exposed to the ins and outs of the fishing industry from a young age. I watched my family work tirelessly to provide fresh, wild-caught seafood to consumers, and I learned to appreciate the hard work and dedication it takes to maintain a sustainable fishing operation.

As I grew older, my passion for fishing and my desire to make a positive impact on the food industry grew stronger. I realized that the global food system had become disconnected from its roots, and I was determined to change that. I wanted to reconnect people with real food and with the hardworking fishermen who provide it. This desire to make a difference ultimately led me to found Fishermen Direct Seafood.

In addition to our commitment to sustainability and transparency, we also place a strong emphasis on community. We believe that by supporting local fishermen and providing fresh, wild-caught seafood to our customers, we can strengthen the connections between people and their food. Our goal is to create a community of like-minded individuals who share our passion for real, sustainable food.

How Does Fisherman Direct Work?

Fishermen Direct Seafood is an online retailer of sustainably sourced, wild-caught seafood. Customers can browse the selection of products on the company's website, select the items they want to purchase, and have them delivered to their doorstep or available for local pickup.

Who Are The Fishermen?

Fishermen Direct Seafood works with independent Alaskan fishermen who are committed to sustainable fishing practices. The company has longstanding relationships with these fishermen, and many have been supplying Fishermen Direct Seafood with high-quality seafood for years.

Learn More >

Where Is The Fish From?

Fishermen Direct Seafood's seafood is wild-caught from the waters of Alaska, which are known for their abundance of high-quality seafood.

Learn More >

How Are The Fish Caught

Fishermen Direct Seafood's fishermen use sustainable and environmentally responsible fishing methods to catch the seafood. These methods vary depending on the type of fish, but all are designed to minimize the impact on the environment and preserve the health of the fish populations.
