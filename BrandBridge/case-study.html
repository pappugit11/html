TIMBERLAND

International, responsive multilingual website

  • Industry: Fashion
  • Web Development
  • Location: London

Timberland, a leading fashion retailer, engaged our team to create a transnational portal, spanning six countries across the Asia-Pacific region.

The Project

It was essential that the design, user journey and UX reflected the US version of the Timberland site, however it needed to be localised for the APAC countries.

The Solution

Our team focused on ensuring that each portal operated responsively, adapting to the domestic language with personalised content and images, yet still promoting Timberland’s brand values and tone of voice. Our Solution Development team worked closely with Timberland’s Singapore unit to scope, develop and build the site. The project was built in WordPress, with multi-site management for the six different sites ensuring localisation for individual countries.

The Future

Our team were responsible for the entire build, providing the technical skills and support needed to successfully deliver the project. Keen to keep our team involved moving forward, Timberland opted for a SLA whereby we remain committed to continuously integrating new features, pages and content into the portal.

Brand Bridge Summary

  • We delivered a fully responsive website that accurately reflects and complements the US parent site, whilst providing localised content.
  • We provided full support and on-going maintenance.
