About Dr. Pahk

Dr. Pahk is a Los Angeles County native. He graduated cum laude with B.S. in Biology at the University of California, San Diego. After spending time working in the field of biotechnology, he graduated from the Technion Institute of Technology in Israel, one of the top technical institutes in the world. He then went on to complete his residency in Neurology at Northwell Health, the largest health system in New York before returning to California...
