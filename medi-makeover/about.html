Your Journey, Our Pleasure

Since 2006...

Medi Makeovers is an Australian-based company that began in 2006 and our first desire is to enable people who choose surgery or dental treatments overseas to have a safe, secure experience that is of the highest standards. Due to our thorough research and experience, we have taken the fear of the unknown out of the equation. One of the main reasons for having surgery in Thailand – and with the hospitals and professionals we have chosen, is the high standards of cosmetic/dental surgery and care they offer. All the accommodation, resorts and spas we offer are industry leaders and won't disappoint.

  • We have 20,000 followers and a 4.9 rating on Facebook
  • We work directly with our surgeons and medical staff, no via 3rd parties
  • No agency fees. No lock-in fees. No hidden fees. All upfront and transparent
  • Over 10,000 clients have taken their journey with us since 2006

Meet Your Surgeon

Dr. Teerapat Jaiprasat

Dr Teerapat Jaiprasat is an internationally renowned Thai, American and Korean trained and qualified cosmetic plastic and reconstructive surgeon. Along with his team of experienced professionals, he operates in his own 5-star hospital standards medical centre and also at many hospitals in Bangkok. He is an extremely passionate surgeon and loves his profession. His skill in suturing is well known to achieve the best scar results. Outstanding Skill with Artistic Vision.
