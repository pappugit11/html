About Us

Medi Makeovers is an Australian-based company that began in 2006 and our first desire is to enable people who choose surgery or dental treatments overseas to have a safe, secure experience that is of the highest standards. Due to our thorough research and experience, we have taken the fear of the unknown out of the equation.

  • 100% Australian Owned & Operated
  • 5-Star service and treatment
  • No agency fees or 'lock-in' fees
  • Ongoing support, you will not be alone.

Your Medi Makeover Procesess

Contact Us

You can contact us via our website, email, Facebook or Instagram or give us a call. If you know which procedures you are interested in, one of our experienced consultants will send you a free quote.

1

Free Medical Assessment

Depending on what you are interested in, surgery or dental or both, you will have to complete the online medical form. You will also be required to submit photos for your free surgical or dental assessment.

2

Date Confirmation

Once you are happy with your surgical or dental assessment, simply let us know the date you would like to book for you procedure. Once your date is confirmed, the travel arrangements will begin - flights, hotels, travel coordinator, etc.

3

The New You

Upon arrival in Thailand, you will be met by one of our coordinators who will take care of the entire process - airport pick up, hotel transfer, meet with the surgeon, private hospital care. Finally, our surgeon will examine you to ensure you are safe to fly home.

4

Ongoing Support

Before, during and after your procedure(s) - your Medi Makeovers consultant will be in touch with you to ensure your recovery and overall experience is as good as can be! Our team prides in their work and wants you to be happy!
