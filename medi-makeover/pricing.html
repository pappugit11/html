Fly Back Cover

Worried about surgery or dental overseas? We offer a Fly Back Cover to ease all your fears! Medi Makeovers offers Fly Back Cover for extra peace of mind for your surgery or dental.

We strongly suggest if you chose to have any procedure, home or abroad you should always take out this type of cover.

As there is risk associated with all types of surgery or dental procedures, Revision Surgery or dental procedures may become necessary following your return home from Thailand.

Our clinics and hospitals in Thailand each have policies in place in the event future revision surgery in Thailand becomes necessary. Where Revision Surgery or dental treatment is required, and is as a direct result of the hospitalisation and/or surgery or dental in Thailand, the hospital/clinic will offer a partial or full contribution towards the costs of revision surgery.

Flights and Luxury Accommodation: Covered.

Flights (for 1 person), accommodation ( for up to 2 persons) and associated travel costs covered, when the hospital has acknowledged clear evidence of revision surgery/dental being required as a result of hospitalisation and/or surgery and/or treatment at their facility. Breakfast is also included with all accommodation.

Capsular Contraction: Covered.

For implants less than 500cc. Capsular Contraction is not covered by hospital surgery revision policies, however, for Medi Makeovers Clients who take out the Dental/Surgery Fly Back Cover – Capsular Contraction is included in your cover.

Transfers: Covered.

All associated transfers in Thailand, airport/hotel/hospital, are all covered.
