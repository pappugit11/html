About Us

About us

We are a group of surgical trainees who are passionate about education. We firmly believe that anyone with the right resources and material, anyone can pass surgical entrance examinations at the first attempt.

We have created this website based upon our own experiences of tutoring junior colleagues and our collective experience of UK surgical examinations.

Through these questions we have collated high yield questions to ensure that we convey the most important topics for the MRCS part B examination. We welcome any feedback and we wish you the very best in your examination.

Omar Musbahi

Trauma and Orthopaedic Registrar and NIHR Academic Clinical Fellow at Imperial
College London
