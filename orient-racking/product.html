Pallet racking

Orient Pallet Racking Frames are available in capacities from Standard to Heavy with nominal load capacities from 8,000kg to 12,000kg.

Five Standard Stock Heights from 3000 to 10000mm and two standard Widths 900mm and 1100mm.

The frames are of bolted construction, and can be delivered to site in either KD form or pre-assembled.

All frames are punched with two rows of 25.4mm coffin slots set at 76.2mm centres 38mm apart, offering an adjustability of the beams of 76.2mm (3").

The beams are formed from a double C section mild steel and fitted with three or four hook connectors welded at either end, all the beams are fully adjustable on a 3" increment, and can be adjusted at any time without the need for either tools or skilled labour.

They are all fitted with automatic safety locks, which means that it is not possible to accidentally dislodge a beam during normal operations with a fork lift truck.
