About Us

Know about SpeakMD!

SpeakMD is a group of highly qualified physicians that understand the complex world of medicine can be overwhelming and confusing for patients struggling at their time of sickness and injury. Our Mission is to provide patients with as much information as possible to empower them on their path to recovery. We do this by translating medical documents into language that every patient can understand so they can better navigate the healthcare system and improve their health.

SpeakMD is, at its core, doctors helping patients. Our physicians took an oath to help and protect patients. We feel this is a way to get back to our orignal calling to medicine while protecting our patients from the complex healthcare system. As more of medicine is shifted to patients managing their own healthcare, we are determined to help patients better understand the medical speak and confusing concepts associated with their health.
