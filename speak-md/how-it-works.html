How it Works

our work process

SpeakMD is an easy to use interface designed to help you get the best information regarding your health.

Obtain your medical documents from your doctor or hospital

(these can include operative reports, insurance explanation of benefits, clinic notes, radiology reports, lab test reports or doctors notes)

Edit your documents to remove any identifying information

See our template on removing your personal information on our getting started page.

Submit your documents to SpeakMD

Simply email the documents to contact@speakmd.com or attach your documents to your order on the checkout page. We accept documents as PDF or take a photo of your documents, attach them and we will convert to PDF for translating.

Receive your documents

You will receive your original submission along with an easy to understand non medical translation that empowers you to take charge of your health.
