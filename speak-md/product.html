Fees and payments

At SpeakMD we understand medical bills can pile up and we do not want to add to that. The physicians of SpeakMD have busy clinical practices seeing many patients like yourself. It takes time, commitment and expertise to provide medical translation. To that end, we do request a fee for the time spent by our physicians reading and translating your documents. See our fee schedule below.

  • Operative Report Translation - $39.99
  • X-ray, CT and MRI Translation - $29.99
  • EOB ( Explanation of Benefits) Translation - $29.99
  • Doctors Notes Translation - $29.99
  • Medical Vocabulary Translation - $.99/Word
