What company is disclosing confidential information?

Enter the name and address of the company that owns and will disclose the confidential information (the "Owner"). Also include the name of the person who will sign the Agreement on behalf of the Owner. It is recommended that a professional title be included. For example: "ABC Company" "John Smith" "CEO".

Who will be receiving the confidential information?

Who will receive the confidential information?

Enter the name and address of the person who will be receiving the confidential information (the "Recipient"). Example: "John Smith." Tell me more.

Why will the Confidential Information be disclosed to the Recipient?

Use complete sentences. For example, "Information will be disclosed to the Recipient to determine whether the Recipient could assist the Owner with the development of a marketing plan." Tell me more.

Will this Agreement include a non-circumvention clause?

"Non-circumvention" means the Recipient cannot do business with the Owner's business contacts without permission from the Owner. Tell me more.

Will the Recipient be required to protect the confidential information after the term of the Non-Disclosure Agreement ends?

Tell me more.

How long will the Recipient be required to protect the confidential information after the term of the Agreement?
