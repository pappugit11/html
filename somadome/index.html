self : soma

dome : the space

The vision for the dome came in a dream that changed my life. The message received was that there needed to be a space, an access point - to go within.

The simplicity of being able to just ‘sit’ with ourselves, to quiet the noise for even just a short moment - has become almost impossible.

Time is moving so fast. The world has become so complex, social media, all the chaos and distractions that seem to have overtaken our mind space - we need to find a way to reconnect within.

The places the dome can go are infinite. It’s a space, an access portal to self located when and where it’s needed most.
